Welcome to the WAVE2WEB Reservoir Watch Hack ! We are providing all teams with a data package to kick-start the research. Teams are encouraged to incorporate additional open-source data into their projects.

Within this Github, you'll find:
  • Daily reservoir water data for the 4 select reservoirs
  • Key contextual shapefiles
  • Aqueduct water risk indicators
